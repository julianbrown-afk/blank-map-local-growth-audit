Referral path

Three steps keep the handoff clean.

The goal is to let the owner self-qualify before anyone asks them to buy. A useful result makes the paid audit easier to understand and easier to deliver.

01 Send the free scorecard

Use the broad scorecard, a category scorecard, or the resource directory depending on how specific the conversation already is.

02 Ask for the result link

The result link preserves the selected checks, score, planning math, and public context without including the visitor email.

03 Bridge only when gaps show up

If the result shows enough friction, send the method page, sample report, or paid audit link. If it does not, keep it helpful and free.

What not to do

Keep partner referrals specific and compliant.

Do not turn this into cold spam.

Use warm introductions, allowed posts, relevant replies, and existing owner conversations. Cold commercial email requires a compliant public mailing address and other legal requirements.

Do not promise revenue.

The audit identifies observable friction and practical fixes. Revenue depends on demand, offer quality, execution, response speed, pricing, and follow-through.

Do not promise a referral fee from this page.

This page is a sharing resource, not a partner contract. Any paid referral arrangement needs separate terms before anyone relies on it.

Do not send private access or passwords.

The audit starts from public website, profile, review, call-to-action, tracking-cue, and follow-up observations. No private logins are needed for the diagnostic.
