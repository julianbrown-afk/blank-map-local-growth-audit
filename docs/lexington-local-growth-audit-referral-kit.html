Boundaries

Use this without turning it into spam.

Can I use this as cold commercial email?

No. Cold commercial email needs a compliant public mailing address and other legal requirements. Use this page for warm referrals, allowed posts, relevant replies, or conversations where the owner has asked about the topic.

What should I send first?

Send one free resource first: the scorecard for a broad self-check, or the conversion audit page when the conversation is already about website performance.

When should the paid audit link be shared?

Share the paid audit after the owner sees a specific buyer-path problem and wants ranked fixes, tracking notes, and a 30-day action plan.

Does the audit guarantee more revenue?

No. It identifies observable friction and practical fixes. Revenue depends on demand, offer quality, execution, response speed, pricing, and follow-through.
