## Review Checklist

For each prospect, inspect:

- Is one primary booking, call, or quote action visible on mobile?
- Does the Google profile appear complete and current?
- Are reviews recent and easy to find?
- Does the website explain the highest-value service clearly?
- Is there a follow-up path for people who do not book immediately?
- Are calls, forms, and booking clicks plausibly trackable by source?
